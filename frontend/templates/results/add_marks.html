Internal CSV Format

Your CSV should have the following headers exactly:

registration_number, cie1, cie2, cie3, mid_term, assignment, remarks
  • cie1, cie2, cie3: Out of 10
  • mid_term: Out of 20
  • assignment: Out of 10
External CSV Format

Your CSV should have the following headers exactly:

registration_number, external_marks, remarks
  • external_marks: Out of 60
Placement CSV Format

Your CSV should have the following headers exactly (all scores out of 100):

registration_number, dsa, oops, dbms, programming, aptitude, interview, remarks
  • dsa, oops, dbms, programming: Mock exam scores
  • aptitude: Aptitude test score
  • interview: Mock interview score
